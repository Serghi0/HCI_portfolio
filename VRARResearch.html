Resident Evil 7 VR

This is a VR adaptation of the game Resident Evil 7. This is a horror game with a lot of jump scares. It is played on the PlayStation 4 with the help of Playstation VR. The most impressive thing about this VR game is that the jump scares get enhanced to the next level. You really feel like you’re in the game since it’s in first person. You really think twice before you go around a corner.
