VR & AR reflection

Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality is the future of media. Even though I am a fan of VR, I am not a fan of the VR goggles. That is why I think AR is the way to go forward. The most valuable aspect of this topic is that it is made to augment the experience of the user. You don’t need to wear glasses to see the augmented reality. Augmented Reality can bring an extra bit of spice to people’s life by giving extra information to things that people see every day. That way they can be sure of the places they go and the things they see on a daily basis.
