Artificial Creatures reflection

I am not sure what kind of benefit an artificial creature would have on my personal life but during the lecture I did see that it has benefits for other people that are dependent on social contact to have a healthy life. An artificial creature could help them get back on the right track if they fell off the health wagon.

The most valuable aspect of this topic is that the artificial creature can think and act on its own. That is a valuable aspect where the user barely has to have an input to get an output from the artificial creature.
