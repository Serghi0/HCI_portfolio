Exertion Interfaces reflection

I am a big fan of exertion interfaces. Personally, I have owned a Nintendo Wii and currently own a Nintendo Switch. Both of these consoles adapt exertion interfaces into their games through motion detection software in their controllers. That way the user himself is a part of the input, which is amazing.

In the future, I see more technologies adapting exertion interfaces. Not only for gaming but also for daily chores or to get people moving. It could be a great stimulus to get people to exercise who normally wouldn’t since they think it would be boring.

The topic met my expectations since the possibilities with this topic is endless. There is not allot of things you can’t adapt to an exertion interface and that’s what makes it interesting yet challenging.
