VR & AR reflection

As an up-and-coming UX designer, I would love to be able to tackle the challenge of making an interface for a wearable product. I see wearables as a necessary improvement in the day-to-day life of people. Nowadays people are more accepting of people with foreign technological objects on their body, so the possibilities of a wearable can be endless.

Personally, I don’t have wearable technology. Yet. I’m just waiting for the wearable technology to advance far enough so that I can have multiple uses for a wearable.
