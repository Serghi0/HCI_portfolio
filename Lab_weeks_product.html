Product description

The product is a basket exertion game. The objective of the game is to score as much as possible so that the audience can become as big as possible. It is an exertion game because you, the player, have to play a real basketball game where you have to score in real life to be able to score in the game. Thus, your body is the input for the game.

The point of the game is to score as much as possible so that the audience can grow as big as possible. You score in real life so that the digital audience can grow. You have to score from each post so that you can move as much as possible. You also have to try and get the high score of the game.
