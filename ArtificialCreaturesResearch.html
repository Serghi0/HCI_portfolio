Artificial Creatures Research

For the artificial creatures research, I found the project called the T8X. It is based on real spiders and its behavior and movements mirror this. It uses 26 servo motors and is powered by the proprietary Bigfoot Robotics Engine, which contains advanced robotics algorithms for controlling multi-legged walking robots, there are endless possibilities for the types of bio-inspired, realistic movements that the robot can make.

What impresses me most about this product is that you can control the T8X via your smartphone, tablet or computer with simple commands that everybody can understand.

It is relevant to the topic because it is a product that only needs simple commands to function properly. Due to it using the Bigfoot Robotics Engine, it will automatically take care of all the complex mathematical computations in the background, including inverse-kinematics calculations, leg trajectory planning, leg gait coordination, motor control, etc.
