Serghio's HCI portfolio

Welcome to my HCI portfolio website.

My name is Serghio Liqui Lung and I am in my second year of Communication and Multimedia Design at The Hague University. This is my portfolio for the course Human Centered Interfaces. This course was alot of fun and on this portfolio website you'll experience it through my words.

My expectations for this course

My expectations for this course are pretty broad. I expect this course to be more practical than theoretical. I expect us to be busy with all kinds of programming. Furthermore, I expect us to be busy building all kinds of gadgets to reach our goals.

My study goals

I would like to challenge myself during this course. I want to be able to learn as much as possible about programming. Not the good old HTML, CSS or javascript but the programming you need for an Arduino or whatever we will use in this course.
