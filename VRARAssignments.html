VR & AR Assignment

Sports/ movements

A Virtual Reality world where the user has to go through an obstacle course. This would be a semi-realistic environment where the user has to go through different obstacles to reach his goal. The game would consist of different environment levels and different difficulty levels. This would make the game have a replay value which would entice the player to play the game more often. The different environment levels would consist of an urban terrain, natural terrains such as jungle or beaches and other world terrains like an alien planet.

VR & AR Assignment

Education and/or research

There could be a virtual reality environment where children could learn subjects in an interactive and engaging way. They could learn about geography for example where they would travel to a virtual copy of a country and learn about different things from that country. It could also be adapted for history where students would be transferred to a specific time period. Here they would be able to interact with historical figures and learn from them.

VR & AR Assignment

(anxiety) therapy

There could be an anxiety therapy virtual reality simulator where the user could learn how to control their anxiety. They will be confronted with situations from a third person perspective and show solutions for each situation, so they can learn and adapt this approach to their own approach. If they are ready for it, they will be able to confront these situations from a first-person perspective. Here they will be able to get options to tackle these situations so that they still would have a helping hand.
